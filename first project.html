thirstycrowflew into a village in search of water. The crow flew over the houses, fields, and trees. ... The crow put his beak inside the pitcher
but could not reach the water. The water level was too low, and the narrow opening prevented his neck from going all the way down.

The moral of this story - "Where there is a will there is a way". In this very short story for small kids, once when a clever crow is flying, it feels thirsty. It sees a pot of water, but the water is too low inside the pot for the crow to reach.
